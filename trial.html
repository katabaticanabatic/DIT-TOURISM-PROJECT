Murchison Falls National Park

Murchison Falls National Park is known for its diverse wildlife including elephants, giraffes, lions, and hippos. The park is also home to the breathtaking Murchison Falls where the Nile River forces its way through a narrow gorge.

Bwindi Impenetrable National Park

Bwindi Impenetrable National Park is famous for its population of endangered mountain gorillas. It is also a UNESCO World Heritage Site and offers stunning biodiversity with numerous species of birds, primates, and plants.

Queen Elizabeth National Park

Queen Elizabeth National Park is renowned for its diverse ecosystems, which include savanna, wetlands, and forests. It is home to various wildlife, including elephants, lions, and chimpanzees.
